# Business Overview

## Company Description

SynthoMatrixx Technologies is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) provider delivering identity management, compliance, workflow automation and cybersecurity solutions to enterprise organisations throughout North America and the Caribbean.

The company develops cloud-native applications that assist organisations in securing digital identities, protecting sensitive information and improving operational efficiency.

---

## Customers

Primary customers include:

- Financial institutions
- Healthcare providers
- Government agencies
- Educational institutions
- Enterprise businesses

---

## Business Model

SynthoMatrixx operates under a subscription-based SaaS model with customers purchasing annual or monthly licences.

Support services include:

- Technical Support
- Identity Management
- Infrastructure Monitoring
- Security Operations
- Professional Services
