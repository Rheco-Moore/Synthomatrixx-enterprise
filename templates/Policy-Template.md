## Purpose

To ensure secure access to organizational systems and data by enforcing identity and access management controls.

---

## Scope

Applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party users accessing company systems, applications, and cloud services.

---

## Responsibilities

- IT Operations: Implement and monitor access controls.
- Security Team: Review and audit access logs.
- Employees: Follow access procedures and report anomalies.

---

## Policy Statement

- All accounts must use multi-factor authentication (MFA).
- Access is granted based on least privilege.
- Role-based access controls (RBAC) must be applied.

---

## Exceptions

Any exceptions must be documented, approved by the Security Officer, and reviewed quarterly.

---
